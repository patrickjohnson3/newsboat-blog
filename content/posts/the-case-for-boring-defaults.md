date: 2026-02-03
readTime: "4 min"

---

Defaults are where products go to be judged. Loud defaults feel like arrogance.

A boring default gives users confidence. It is the quiet handshake that says: you are safe here.

When in doubt, choose the setting that keeps data, reduces noise, and lowers surprise.
