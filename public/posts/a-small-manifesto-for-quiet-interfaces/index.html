A Small Manifesto for Quiet Interfaces
Jan 12, 2026 - design / product

News readers are built for flow: scan, select, dive, return. In a world of infinite scrolling and decorative chrome, the calm interface is a radical choice.

This blog is an experiment in restraint: no popups, no attention traps, just clean type and a fast way in and out.

When interfaces are quiet, content feels louder. It lets your thoughts meet the text without fighting the UI.

So today, here’s the manifesto: give the reader a path. Make the path reliable. Let the words do the work.
